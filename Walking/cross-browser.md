
IE8, not supported:

* CSS3 lengths (`rem`, etc)
* CSS3 colors (rgba, hsl, hsla)
* pseudo-elements `:` vs `::`
* border-radius
* SVG
* opacity (filter hack)
* transform
* transition (also IE9)
* animation (also IE9)
